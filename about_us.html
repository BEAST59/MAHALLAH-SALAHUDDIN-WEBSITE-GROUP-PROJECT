About Us

Nestled within the academic landscape of IIUM, Mahallah Salahuddin al Ayyubi stands as a unique residential college tailored for students actively engaged in various uniformed bodies, including the Army, Airforce, SUKSIS (Student Security), and SISPA (Student Police). This distinctive residence hall embodies the spirit of discipline and service, providing an exclusive space for those committed to the rigorous demands of uniformed services.


The essence of Mahallah Salahuddin al Ayyubi lies not only in its physical structure but in the sense of camaraderie and shared purpose that permeates its halls. As a dual-gender residential facility, the college fosters an inclusive environment, breaking away from traditional gender-segregated models. This deliberate integration aims to cultivate mutual respect and understanding between students from diverse backgrounds, fostering a supportive community that transcends gender boundaries.


The residents of Mahallah Salahuddin al Ayyubi are not merely inhabitants; they are part of a community where discipline meets diversity. The college is designed to meet the specific needs of students engaged in uniformed services, offering specialized facilities and resources to support their demanding schedules. From dedicated study areas to physical fitness spaces tailored for military training, the infrastructure is carefully crafted to complement the holistic development of individuals dedicated to service and leadership.


In conclusion, Mahallah Salahuddin al Ayyubi stands as a beacon within IIUM, embodying a commitment to discipline, service, and inclusive community living. This dual-gender residential college provides a home for students engaged in uniformed bodies, fostering an environment where the values of duty and diversity harmoniously coexist. Through its unique approach to residential living, Mahallah Salahuddin al Ayyubi shapes not only individuals but a collective spirit that resonates with the ethos of service and unity.
